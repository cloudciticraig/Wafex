Signature Programs

Exclusive genetics you won't find elsewhere.

Helix™ Waxflower & Boronia

Bred on Wafex farms in WA & Victoria since 2006

In 2006, Craig Musson and Adrian Parsons founded breeding company Helix Australia to secure the future of Australia's most popular commercial native flower. From trial farms at Regan's Ford (WA) and Longford (VIC), a continual pipeline of new waxflower varieties reaches the market — grown under licence around the world.

Exclusive to Wafex & licensed growers

Ayoba™ Proteaceae

Best-in-class African & Australian natives

A curated protea, leucospermum and leucadendron program built on superb farm relationships, improved packaging and stabilised pricing — bringing order to a famously wild category. Exclusive distribution in North America through Wafex USA.

Quality & Care

Picked at dawn, graded by hand, flown overnight.

Thirty years of export logistics means Wafex sweats the unglamorous details that decide whether a flower delights or disappoints.

  • Unbroken cold chain

    Purpose-built coldrooms in every facility, from West Swan to our 2,000 m² Brisbane coldroom, keep stems at optimal temperature door to door.

  • QA at origin and arrival

    Every consignment is inspected against documented quality standards — stem length, bloom stage, hydration and pack integrity.

  • Ethics you can trace

    Fairtrade-aligned sourcing in Africa and long-term family-farm relationships everywhere else. We know every grower by name.
